About UnScript2k18

It is a 24hrs Hackathon with problem Statement from Web and Android Domain. UnScript is a platform for young developer to shine in these domains without fee constraints and lots of food during the journey.

What is Our Goal?

Web and Android apps are integrated part of every human being these days.. The web and Android apps therefore need to be secure user friendly and light on storage. UnScript2k18 aims to build such solutions to problem statements and also helps to discover young developers
